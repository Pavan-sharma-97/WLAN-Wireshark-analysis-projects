# 🔍 Probe Request (Subtype 4)

Sent by STA to discover APs.

---

## Addresses
| Address | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Address1 | Broadcast FF:FF:FF:FF:FF:FF |
| Address2 | STA |
| Address3 | Broadcast |

---

## Fields
- SSID (May be empty = wildcard probe)
- Supported Rates
- Extended Supported Rates
- HT Capabilities
- VHT Capabilities
